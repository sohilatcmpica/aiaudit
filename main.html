The Diagnostic
What the audit
will uncover.
Each gap is a finding. Each finding becomes a conversation. Each conversation becomes a proposal. This is the commercial logic of the AI Audit.
01

No Lead Management System

Enquiries arrive through WhatsApp, Instagram DMs, calls, and walk-ins — tracked in someone's head or a messy group chat. No CRM, no follow-up schedule, no conversion visibility.

02

Manual, Reactive Customer Service

All queries handled one by one, during business hours only. No FAQ automation, no chatbot. Quality depends entirely on which staff member picks up the message.

03

No Automated Follow-Up or Nurture

After first contact or purchase, nothing happens automatically. No thank-you, no upsell, no re-engagement. Customers drift away. Reactivation costs far more than retention.

04

Disconnected Data, Zero Analytics

Sales in one system, marketing in another, operations in a third. No unified view. Reports produced manually are outdated before they're ready. Decisions made without real data.

05

Entirely Manual HR & Onboarding

Hiring through WhatsApp referrals with no structured screening. Verbal onboarding. No digital training portal. Every new hire costs weeks of senior management time.

06

No Marketing Automation

Social media posts done when someone remembers. Email marketing sporadic. No content calendar, no scheduled campaigns, no measurement of what works. Marketing is reactive and unmeasured.

07

Slow, Error-Prone Finance & Admin

Invoicing, expense tracking, and reconciliation done manually in Excel or paper. Errors frequent. VAT filing stressful. Payment reminders sent manually — or simply forgotten.

08

No Knowledge Management or SOPs

Processes live in people's heads. When someone leaves, knowledge leaves with them. No documented procedures. Training is expensive, slow, and inconsistent every single time.

Answering the same questions

"What are your hours?" "Do you deliver?" — answered 50 times daily by people who could be doing real work.

Manual data entry

Copying orders from WhatsApp into spreadsheets, re-typing customer details across systems — every day without fail.

Creating invoices & quotes

Built from scratch, sent manually, tracked by memory, reminders sent one contact at a time.

Scheduling appointments

Back-and-forth WhatsApp conversations to find a time slot. Manual calendar entry. Reminder calls the day before.

Following up with leads

Remembering to follow up at day 3, 7, 14 — entirely dependent on a person's memory and discipline.

Writing the same emails

Welcome, confirmation, enquiry, complaint — each written from scratch, every single time.

Weekly & monthly reports

Pulling numbers from multiple places, building the same spreadsheet, presenting data that's already outdated.

Social media posting

Creating content and posting manually — or scrambling when someone finally remembers it hasn't been done.

Inventory counting & ordering

Manual stocktakes, guessing reorder points, calling suppliers while checking spreadsheets. Errors everywhere.

Employee onboarding

Collecting documents, filling forms, explaining policies, setting up accounts — every hire pulls a manager for days.

Shift scheduling

Building rosters manually, swap requests over WhatsApp, chasing confirmations, last-minute changes by phone.

Collecting feedback

Asking for reviews individually, reading feedback manually, writing reports nobody has time to act on.

Finance & Admin

+
Before

Staff manually enter transactions, generate invoices, chase payments, prepare reports — with frequent errors. VAT season is a crisis. Cash flow is a mystery.

After

HR & People

+
Before

Hiring, onboarding, and HR run through the founder. If unavailable, everything stops. Interviewing is intuition-based. Onboarding is verbal. Staff leave because nothing is formalised.

After

AI handles CV screening, onboarding sequences, staff HR queries, leave and attendance — all automated. The founder is no longer the bottleneck. The business runs without them being present in every decision.
