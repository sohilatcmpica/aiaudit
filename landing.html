Questions Answered
Everything you're probably
wondering right now.

Is this really free? What's the catch?

+
Yes — genuinely free, with no hidden cost or obligation. The audit is our way of starting a relationship the right way. If the report identifies opportunities that Grovia can help with, we may propose our services. But the report is yours regardless, and you are under zero obligation to proceed with us or anyone else.

How long does it take from start to getting my report?

+
The process is: fill the form (5 minutes), attend the discovery call (30 minutes), then receive your report within 5 working days. Total time commitment from you: approximately 35 minutes of your time. Everything else happens on our side.

Do I need to know anything about AI to get value from this?

+
No. The audit is designed for business owners and decision-makers, not technical people. We translate everything into plain language — time saved, money saved, revenue gained. You will never be expected to understand the technology itself, only the business outcome it produces.

My business is small — is AI even relevant for me?

+
This is one of the most common misconceptions we encounter. AI is often more impactful for small businesses than large ones — because small businesses typically have fewer people doing more manual work, and every hour saved matters more. Businesses with as few as 5 employees regularly find 10–15 hours of automatable time per week in their operations.

What happens after I receive my report?

+
You receive the report and it's yours to do with as you choose. We'll follow up once to see if you have questions about the findings. If any of the recommended solutions are something you'd like Grovia to help implement, we're happy to discuss that. If not, the report is still valuable on its own — many clients use it to brief their own internal teams or other vendors.

Is my business information kept confidential?

+
Completely. All information shared during the intake form and the discovery call is kept strictly confidential and used only to prepare your audit report. We operate under standard NDA conditions for all client conversations. Your data is never shared with third parties.

We already use some software tools — will this still be useful?

+
Yes, often more so. Knowing what tools you currently use helps us identify integration opportunities, inefficiencies in your existing stack, and gaps between what your tools can do and how you're actually using them. Businesses with existing software often find the highest-impact AI opportunities are in automating the connections between tools they already have.

How is this different from a generic AI consultation?

+
Most AI consultations give you a general overview of what AI can do. Ours gives you a scored assessment of what AI can do specifically for your business, in your industry, at your scale, with your current team and tools. The output is not a presentation about AI trends — it is a personalised action plan with specific recommendations and ROI projections tied to your actual operation.
